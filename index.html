About Me

I'm a passionate developer who loves bringing ideas to life — whether it's creating fun games, building practical everyday tools, or exploring the latest tech trends. Technology has always fascinated me, and I thrive on finding new ways to make it accessible, exciting, and useful.

I believe in combining creativity with problem-solving to build experiences that make a real difference. Whether it's through a playful game, a productivity app, or a unique solution to a business need, my goal is to craft projects that are both meaningful and innovative.

  • Designing fun and engaging games that spark joy and creativity.

  • Creating random projects for fun and experimenting with new ideas.

Technical Proficiencies

A look at the programming languages and technologies I’m passionate about and the tools I use to bring ideas to life.

Python

Python was the first official programming language I learned, and it quickly showed me how simple and powerful coding could be. Its readability and versatility made it the perfect foundation for my development journey, and I continue to rely on it for solving complex problems efficiently.

C#

C# has become my favorite language to work with. I love how it allows me to craft dynamic desktop applications and games, blending technical structure with creativity. It’s a language that feels both powerful and intuitive, making it a key part of my current projects.

Java

Java was my introduction to building forms and interactive applications. It opened the door to creating polished, user-friendly software with a strong backend, and showed me how structured coding can bring ideas to life in a clean, scalable way.

HTML

HTML’s straightforward structure and creative flexibility make it one of my favorite tools. Designing web pages from scratch lets me scratch that creative itch while keeping the code clean, organized, and visually engaging.

JavaScript

JavaScript took my web development skills to the next level. It gave life to my HTML projects, allowing me to add interactivity and dynamic features easily. I found its logic approachable and fun, which made learning it a natural and exciting experience.

COBOL

COBOL taught me the importance of organization and attention to detail. Its strict rules and structure made me a more disciplined coder, and gave me an appreciation for the legacy systems that are still critical to industries today.
